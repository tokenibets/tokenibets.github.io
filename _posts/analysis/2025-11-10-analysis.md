## Key Lessons

* **Heat filter for 3s:** Unders for 3&D guards/wings (e.g., **Donovan Mitchell**, **De’Andre Hunter**) remain sharp; bake into models.
* **Harden iso is alive:** Teammate overs become fragile unless mispriced; ATL wings are elite stoppers and create weird rebound trees (e.g., **Jalen Johnson**).
* **Knueppel is real:** Hornets props generally inflated, but **Knueppel** minutes + role are anchor-worthy; **Smart** resurgence is nudging his numbers up—expect regression to mean.
* **LaRavia & Rui first-bucket:** Still viable; variance hurt today—odds may lengthen (good).
* **Ace Bailey trend:** Higher-motor, rim-attacking archetype unlocking; keep buying modest ladders.
* **Pels rookies (Fears/Queen):** Counting stats possible, but team defense springy—attack opposing matchups vs them.
* **Banchero endurance:** Don’t auto-fade on B2B; price-sensitivity matters. Watch **Avdija** and **Bane** assist overs as self-creation draws doubles.
* **Buzelis:** Asset for CHI, but hasn’t earned locked minutes—cap exposure.
* **MIL–DAL script:** Read was close; **Flagg** = real usage; **AJ Green** role stable; **Giannis AST** live when others convert.
