## Overall Lessons 

* **Anti-Heliocentric F/C Defense (Wemby, Giannis, etc.):** **Guard/wing on permieter→ big double inside the arc** is spreading; expect **lower pts** and spikier turnover rates (and possibly higher assists if the F/C is a talented passer).
* **Board Distribution with Elite Boxing-Out Centers:** Trend toward **fast-twitch guards/wings vulturing rebounds from bigs** (see **DEN**). Scan for similar teams (**HOU with Şengün, PHI with Embiid**) to find edge in **guard/wings REB overs**, or conversely, **forward/center REB unders**.
* **Creation Scarcity Flags:** **Mavs'** and **Wizards'** lack of point guards suppress clean **C&S 3PT** ecosystems; fade volume-dependent shooters there unless pricing compensates (e.g., Klay Thompson only getting 7FGA despite shooting efficiently at 79% TS, or Tre Johnson 2 3FGA despite his elite shooting profile).
* **Kings Validation:** As expected, **Schroder and Westbrook** bring veteran backcourt presence to an already talented Kings team with 18/7/7 and 23/16/10 respectively; **Nique Clifford and Maxime Raynaud** also prove themselves on court in a close-fought game, notching 33 and 15 minutes, respectively.
* **Rookie Performances:** **VJ Edgecombe** falters hard, shooting 3/14 FG and 0/5 3FG; Florida alum **Will Richard** puts up 30/7/3 on 83% TS; **Egor Demin** proves unowrthy of the rock with 0/3/2 on 0/6 FG; **Ace Bailey** shows flashes of returning to form, shooting 3/6 FG for 9/4/1 in 19 minutes.
