## November 10 — Bets & Rationale

**CLE vs MIA (CLE -7.5)**
**[As of 5pm 11-10-25]**

Cleveland are three-possession favorites against a Miami group riding an emotional win over the Lakers. If the Cavs don’t blitz early, expect **Mitchell**, **Norm**, and **Ware** to clear 30+ minutes, with **JJJ** close behind. Cleveland’s backcourt—**Garland**, **Mitchell**, **Hunter**—should each see 30+ as well, with **Merrill**, **Tyson**, and **Ball** mixing in. The Cavs’ edge on the glass (via **Mobley**/**Allen**) muddies rebounding props, nudging this toward **PA/3PT** angles.

For Miami, **Davion Mitchell**’s usage/creation looks sturdy; **Wiggins** profiles as an elite wing option; **Norm** projects for **8+ 3PA**. One of **Garland/Don** likely draws the tougher Davion matchup defensively, while whichever Cav guard catches **Norm**’s assignment benefits most offensively. To lighten the burden, expect **Hunter** and **Mobley** to step into more threes.

Depth notes: **Pelle Larsson**, **Wiggins**, **Ware** for Miami; **JJJ**, **Jović**, **Fontecchio**, **Dru Smith** in the mix; **Nance Jr.**, **Merrill**, **Lonzo**, **Dean Wade**, **Proctor**, **Tyson** for Cleveland.
